Cohab


Cohab is a cohabitation service to help couples start their first homes together.

At Cohab, we aim to make live-in easier and stress free for couples. We value ourselves in providing an enjoyable, memorable and loving experience to our customers.


Skills Developed: Customer Journey Mapping, Information Experience, Service Design

Team size: 4

My Role: Product Management, UX Design.

Tools used: Sketch, IDEO cards, Keynote.

Overview



Value Proposition

Cohabitation Service: Helping couples start their first homes together.

Need: Make live-in easier and stress free.

Differentiator: Caters to the needs of loving and committed couples who want to move in. The service provides tools to make this important journey in a relationship kind, honest, and fun!

Values: Enjoyable, memorable, loving, ease, open communication.

Key findings


  • 11 out of 11 people interviewed told that they moved in with their partners to further their relationship and spend more time with their partners.
  • 8 out of 11 felt that one of the partners ended up making bigger compromises.
  • Small problems, when not communicated and resolved, can lead to a stressful home life.
  • Sharing household chores, different eating habits and not having enough personal space are the major challenges faced by couples living together.

User Persona


Mark and Lexie want to move in together, but they are nervous that they are not ready. They ask for advice, but help they get is not great. Then they hear about the cohabitation service and decide to give it a try.

Design Process


Sketched 8 ideas each & 3 rounds of braiding ~ 33 ideas. Narrowed by grouping ideas into categories.

Customer Journey Mapping


  • Mark and Lexie come across Cohab advertisment on Instagram.
  • They get excited and visit the Cohab Website.


  • The users are prompted to take a quiz in order for us to get them to know better and customise their starter kit.


  • The couples are taken to a short and fun quiz, which is meant to be taken separately.


  • The users are then asked to Create an Account to get their quiz results.


  • We take few moments to come up with specialised test results as our customers wait in anticipation.
  • The users then purchase the toolkit for move-in. Cohab dispatches the specialised toolkit to the couple's new home. The contents of the toolkit are based on the quiz results. For example, Mark and Lexie are 'Foodie Couple' who love to cook. So, their kit contains of cookbooks, chocolates and a Wine bottle.

Usability Testing


  • Our usability testing participants liked the short-fun quiz.
  • Some of our participants didn't like the idea of sending the quiz link to the other partner. They thought it might look like a spam mail.
  • They loved the idea of making move-in a celebrated event in their lives.

Future Work

In the future work, we are planning to provide users with a seamless information expereince so that they get the right information at the right place.
