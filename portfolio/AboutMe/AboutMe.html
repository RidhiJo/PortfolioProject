About Me

Hey! I'm Ridhi. I live in Seattle, WA. I am currently pursuing my Masters in Information Management, with a focus on software development and user experience from the University of Washington. I am extremely passionate about Product Management because it allows me to combine my interests in Computer Science, UX design and Consumer Behavior.

Outside classroom and work, I am a published author of the book 'Lovedale'. I am also the founder of the Product Management club at the University of Washington. In my free time, I love to read books, drink hot chocolate and walk in the woods. Some of my favourite books are 'Designing your life', 'The perks of being a wallflower' and 'The girl on the train'. This summer, I am interning as a product designer at Hoylu, a Swedish company in Kirkland .
