About Me

Hola! I am Ridhi. I am currently pursuing my Masters in Information Management, with a focus on User Experience from the University of Washington.

I am extremely passionate about Product Management because it allows me to combine my interests in Computer Science, UX design and consumer behavior.

Outside classroom and work, I am a published author of the book 'Lovedale'. I am also the founder of the Product Management club at the University of Washington.

In my free time, I love to read books, drink coffee and take a walk in the woods.
