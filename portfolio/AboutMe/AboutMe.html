About Me

Hello! My name is Ridhi. I live in Seattle, WA. I am currently pursuing my Masters in Information Management, with a focus on User Experience from the University of Washington. I am extremely passionate about Product Management because it allows me to combine my interests in Computer Science, UX design and consumer behavior.

Outside classroom and work, I am a published author of the book 'Lovedale'. I am also the founder of the Product Management club at the University of Washington. In my free time, I love to read books, drink hot chocolate and take a walk in the woods. Some of my favourite books are 'Britt Marie was here', 'The perks of being a wallflower', 'The girl on the train'. I will be interning as a product designer at Hoylu, a Swedish company in Kirkland this summer.
