The Problem

40 percent of all U.S food goes uneaten yearly, resulting in 133 billion pounds wasted. Food wastage is responsible for many social evils such as climatic change and hunger. How do we reduce food wastage at the University Of Washington (UW), by distributing the leftover food that is good for consumption?
